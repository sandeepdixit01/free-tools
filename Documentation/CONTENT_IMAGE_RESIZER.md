**Choose the Right Size**

### H2: Frequently Asked Questions

**Does resizing reduce image quality?**  
Our smart compression minimizes quality loss. While some reduction is necessary to achieve smaller file sizes, our algorithm preserves visual clarity better than standard compression.

**What's the difference between resize and compress?**  
Resizing changes dimensions (width × height), while compression reduces file size. Our tool does both—it adjusts dimensions and applies compression to hit your exact KB target.

**Can I resize multiple images at once?**  
Currently, our tool processes one image at a time. Batch processing is coming soon. For now, you can resize images sequentially in seconds.

**Which format is best for web use?**  
JPG is best for photographs and complex images. PNG works well for graphics, logos, and images requiring transparency. WebP offers superior compression but isn't supported by all browsers.

**Will my images be stored on your servers?**  
No. All processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
